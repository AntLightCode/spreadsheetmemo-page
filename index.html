A powerful flashcard-based learning web app that combines the flexibility of Google Sheets with effective memorization techniques

Key Features

Effective Learning Method

Two-round system that tests your memory and reinforces new knowledge. Track your progress with difficulty ratings: "I knew it" or "I didn't know".

Google Sheets Integration

Store your flashcards in Google Sheets with all the benefits: sharing, editing, printing, and collaborative learning with friends.

Mobile Compatible

Study anywhere, anytime. Works seamlessly on computers, tablets, and smartphones. Just need a web browser!

Rich Content Support

Add images, links, mathematical equations (TeX/AsciiMath), and YouTube videos. Use Wiki Markup for advanced formatting.

Multiple Sheets

Organize content with multiple sheets and named ranges. Perfect for different subjects, topics, or collaborative learning.

Private & Secure

Your documents belong only to you. No personal information collected or shared. All connections via secure HTTPS.

How It Works

Free Forever: No ads, no hidden costs. Completely free for personal and educational use.
  1. Create or Import: Start with a Google Sheet containing your questions and answers, or create a new one through the app.
  2. Authorize Once: Grant SpreadsheetMemo permission to access your spreadsheets (your email and password remain with Google).
  3. Start Learning: Begin your lesson with the two-round system designed for effective memorization.
  4. Track Progress: Monitor your learning with detailed statistics: well known, neutral, troubling, and untouched items.

See It In Action

Dashboard Overview

View all your documents with detailed progress statistics. Track well-known, neutral, troubling, and untouched items at a glance.

Multiple Documents

Manage multiple learning documents. Each shows last activity, total items, and overall progress percentage.

Learning Session

Clean, distraction-free interface. See the question, think about the answer, then reveal it when ready.

Self-Assessment

Rate your knowledge honestly with "I knew it" or "I didn't know" buttons. The app adapts to focus on challenging items.

Quick Add Content

Easily add new flashcards directly from the app. Support for questions, answers, additional info, links, and images.

Lesson Summary

Review your performance after each lesson. See your efficiency rate and decide whether to do another round.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is the top priority. SpreadsheetMemo doesn't collect any personal information, and all your data stays in your Google Drive.
