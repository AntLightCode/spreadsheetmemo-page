Key Features

Effective Learning Method

Two-round system that tests your memory and reinforces new knowledge.

Google Sheets Integration

Store your flashcards in Google Sheets with all the benefits: sharing, editing, printing, and collaborative learning with friends.

Mobile Compatible

Study anywhere, anytime. Works seamlessly on computers, tablets, and smartphones. Just need a web browser!

Rich Content Support

Add images, links, mathematical equations (TeX/AsciiMath), and YouTube videos. Use Wiki Markup for advanced formatting.

Multiple Sheets

Organize content with multiple sheets and named ranges. Perfect for different subjects, topics, or collaborative learning.

Private & Secure

Your documents belong only to you. No personal information collected or shared. All connections via secure HTTPS.

Free Forever: No ads, no hidden costs. Completely free for personal and educational use.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is the top priority. SpreadsheetMemo doesn't collect any personal information, and all your data stays in your Google Drive.
