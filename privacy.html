Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SpreadsheetMemo ("I", "my" or "the app") is a flashcard-based learning web application developed by AntLightCode. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information when you use the service.

2. Information Collected by the App

SpreadsheetMemo app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

SpreadsheetMemo operates entirely through Google's infrastructure and requires the following permissions to function:

  • View and manage your spreadsheets in Google Drive: Required to access and modify your SpreadsheetMemo documents for learning purposes.
  • View and manage files in your Google Drive: Required to locate and open SpreadsheetMemo documents. The app only accesses SpreadsheetMemo documents and has no access to any other files.

3. How SpreadsheetMemo Uses Your Information

The app accesses your Google Sheets documents solely to:

  • Display flashcard content for learning sessions
  • Track your progress within the spreadsheet (marking items as known, unknown, etc.)
  • Allow you to add new flashcard entries

All data remains in your Google Drive and is never transmitted to or stored on any servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All your flashcard data is stored in your own Google Drive account
  • SpreadsheetMemo does not maintain any databases or servers that store user data
  • Your documents belong only to you
  • All connections are made via secure HTTPS protocol

5. Google Authorization

SpreadsheetMemo uses Google's OAuth 2.0 for authentication. This means:

  • Your email, login, password, and personal information are NOT passed to SpreadsheetMemo
  • They remain your property and are managed by Google
  • From the app perspective, you remain anonymous
  • SpreadsheetMemo only receive authorization to access specific Google Drive files on your behalf

6. Third-Party Services

SpreadsheetMemo is built on Google Apps Script platform and uses:

  • Google Sheets API (for spreadsheet access)
  • Google Drive API (for file management)

These services operate under their own privacy policies. Google's privacy policy can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is in your Google Drive and accessible at any time
  • Modification: You can edit your spreadsheets directly in Google Sheets
  • Deletion: You can delete any SpreadsheetMemo documents from your Google Drive
  • Revocation: You can revoke SpreadsheetMemo's access at any time

8. Revoking Access

You can uninstall and revoke SpreadsheetMemo's access in two ways:

  • Visit https://myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove SpreadsheetMemo
  • Use the uninstall link in the email from Google titled "More information about Spreadsheet Memo with uninstall instructions"

Any documents you've created will remain in your Google Drive after uninstalling.

9. Cookies and Tracking

SpreadsheetMemo does not use cookies for tracking purposes. Google may use cookies for authentication and session management as part of their services.

10. Children's Privacy

SpreadsheetMemo can be used for educational purposes by users of all ages. Since the App does not collect any personal information, there are no age restrictions. However, users under 13 should use the service with parental supervision as required by Google's terms of service.

11. International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored in your Google Drive, data storage location is determined by your Google account settings. SpreadsheetMemo itself does not transfer any data internationally.
